<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Note: </strong>Since the anwer can be very large, so return the answer modulo 10<sup>9</sup>+7.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>n = 2
<strong>Output: </strong>6
<strong>Explanation: </strong>There are 6 sequences of length 2*n, the sequences are 0101, 0110, 1010, 1001, 0000 and 1111.</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>n = 1
<strong>Output: </strong>2
<strong>Explanation: </strong>There are 2 sequence of length 2*n, the sequence are 00 and 11.</span>&nbsp;</pre>
